## Garmin tactix

### Overview

This page lists the GNSS chipsets that are used in the Garmin tactix watches.

The tactix is similar to the 51 mm fÄ“nix / epix, but with some additional military, tactical and aviation [features](https://www.garmin.com/en-GB/compare/?compareProduct=865945&compareProduct=802703).

The various models are listed in reverse-chronological order. The release dates are generally from Garmin announcements.
