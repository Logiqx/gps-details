## Broadcom GNSS Chipsets

The BCM4752 family is Broadcom's third-generation multi-constellation GNSS receiver, supporting GPS + GLONASS + QZSS.

### BCM47522

The BCM47522 was used in the [Samsung Galaxy A8](https://84233d3a-c20f-4143-aaf4-2dc9d93ce720.cdn.zimorg.ir/repository/other/1399/03/11/jfo2o0tq.fzq.pdf) (Exynos) which was released in 2016.

It is also mentioned on websites such as [Jotrin Electronics](https://www.jotrin.com/product/parts/BCM47522IUB2G) and [Yole Group](https://www.reverse-costing.com/teardowns/components/?subcategory=GPS&die_count=+Die+number+0+-+60&manufacturer=BROADCOM).

### BCM47521

The [BCM47521](https://web.archive.org/web/20160711042742/http://www.broadcom.com/products/GPS/GPS-Silicon-Solutions/BCM47521) was announced on 20 Feb 2013 in [Electronic Specifier](https://www.electronicspecifier.com/industries/wireless/bcm47521-broadcom-gnss-location-chip-geofence-capabilities/).

The BCM47521 appears in several Samsung phones, tablets, and watches:

- [Samsung Galaxy S4](https://www.techinsights.com/blog/samsung-galaxy-s4-teardown) (Exynos) - Apr 2013
- [Samsung Galaxy S5 Mini](https://www.ifixit.com/Teardown/Samsung+Galaxy+S5+Mini+Teardown/27579) (Exynos) - Jul 2014
- [Samsung Gear Sport](https://gethypoxic.com/blogs/technical/samsung-gear-sport-teardown) - Oct 2017
- Samsung Galaxy Tab Active 2 - Nov 2017

### BCM4752

The [BCM4752](https://web.archive.org/web/20181016063114/https://www.broadcom.com/products/wireless/gnss-gps-socs/bcm4752#overview) was announced on 21 Mar 2012 in [The Verge](https://www.theverge.com/2012/3/21/2890127/broadcom-gps-chip-indoor-positioning-bcm4752), then in Apr 2012 by [Azertac](https://azertag.az/en/xeber/broadcom_introduces_new_gps_chip_offering_a_platform_for_development_of_indoor_positioning_applications-77267) and [Coordinates](https://mycoordinates.org/gnss-receiver-covers-all-multi-constellation-standards/).

An old page from the Broadcom website can be found on the [Wayback Machine](https://web.archive.org/web/20160711042742/http://www.broadcom.com/products/GPS/GPS-Silicon-Solutions/BCM4752).
