## Broadcom GNSS Chipsets

### BCM4750

The BCM4750 is a GPS-only receiver, announced on 4 Jan 2008 in [InsideGNSS](https://insidegnss.com/broadcom-offers-single-die-agps-oem-receiver/).

It appears in several Apple products, including:

- iPad - Apr 2010
- [iPhone 4](https://www.techinsights.com/blog/apple-iphone-4-teardown) - Jun 2010

n.b. This information was sourced from an article at [EE Times](https://www.eetimes.com/samsungs-galaxy-s-smartphone-whats-under-the-amoled/).
