## Broadcom GNSS Chipsets

### BCM4751

#### BCM47511

The BCM4751 is a multi-constellation GNSS receiver, supporting GPS + GLONASS + QZSS.

It was announced on 9 Feb 2011 in [East-West Digitial News](https://www.ewdn.com/2011/02/09/new-broadcom-solutions-double-the-number-of-satellites-available-for-navigation-with-the-addition-of-glonass-satellite-constellation-support/), mentioning support for GLONASS.

The BCM47511 appears in the [Samsung Galaxy S III (Exynos)](https://www.ifixit.com/Teardown/Samsung+Galaxy+S+III+Teardown/9391) of May 2012

#### BCM4751

The BCM4751 is a multi-constellation GNSS receiver, supporting GPS + QZSS.

It was announced on 6 Oct 2010 in [GPS Daily](https://www.gpsdaily.com/reports/Broadcom_Announces_Support_For_New_QZSS_Satellites_Launched_By_Japan_999.html), mentioning support for QZSS.

The BCM4751 appears in several Samsung phones, including:

- [Samsung Galaxy S](https://www.eetimes.com/samsungs-galaxy-s-smartphone-whats-under-the-amoled/)
- Samsung Galaxy S II
