## Broadcom GNSS Chipsets

### BCM4771

The [BCM4771](https://web.archive.org/web/20150411010054/http://www.broadcom.com/products/GPS/GPS-Silicon-Solutions/BCM4771) GNSS for fitness wearables integrates a GNSS receiver with a sensor hub function in a single chip. It seamlessly integrates inputs from accelerometer and altimeter with GPS and GLONASS satellite systems.

It was announced on 20 Feb 2014 in [GPSWorld](https://www.gpsworld.com/broadcom-location-chip-for-wearables-uses-less-power/) and on 24 Feb 2014 in [Electronic Specifier](https://www.electronicspecifier.com/industries/wireless/the-industry-s-first-gnss-soc-for-wearable-devices/).
